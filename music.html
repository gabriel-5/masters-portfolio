——b gunge - menu music (2018)

This album was created as part of my final project whilst studying Popular Music at Goldsmiths. The name "menu music" comes from the initial inspiration of the endlessly looping menu music of DVD and video game menus. The genesis of the idea came from listening to 10-hour looped versions of the Dark Souls save selection menu music, and the Resident Evil 4 typewriter music. I wanted to create a highly artificial digital new-age ambient folk album which embodied the character of these fantastic menus.
